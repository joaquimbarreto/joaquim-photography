JOAQUIM BARRETO.

Joaquim Barreto (b. 1975, Lisbon) is a London-based photographer and film maker. From a multicultural background travelling across Australia and China, Joaquim completed a Master in Fashion Photography in 2005 at the UAL, London College of Fashion. Since then, his interest is focused on Fashion, Portrait, Architecture, Landscape and Daily-life imaging.
